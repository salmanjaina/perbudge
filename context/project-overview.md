# Kharcha — AI-Powered Personal Finance Tracker

## Overview

Kharcha is a full-stack, AI-powered personal finance platform built for Indian households. It is designed for families juggling multiple EMIs, loans, shared expenses, and scattered investments. It moves beyond passive dashboards to provide active decision-making insights.

## Core Value Proposition

1. **Zero-friction input** — Logging a transaction must take under 5 seconds. Natural language parsing, quick-tap categories, or voice. The mobile-first UI must feel native, not like a spreadsheet.
2. **AI that gives decisions, not just charts** — Weekly digests, anomaly alerts, early loan closure plans, and a chat interface to ask financial data questions. 

## Features & Phasing

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Current)
*Goal: Get all financial data in one place with a UI that users enjoy using.*
- **Transaction Input System**: Mobile bottom sheet / web modal. Natural language parsing via Gemini ("paid Rahul 500 for lunch"). Auto-category tagging, split expense support, "Who owes whom" tracker.
- **Money Flow Dashboard**: Monthly cash flow, category breakdown, net worth snapshot, calendar view of upcoming payments.
- **AI Financial Insights**: Weekly digest, spend anomaly alerts, ask-your-data chat, savings nudges.
- **Data Security**: Clerk-managed sessions, full data export, AES encryption for sensitive fields.

### Phase 2 — Intelligence Layer (Planned)
*Goal: Move from tracking to decision-making and future planning.*
- **Loan & EMI Optimizer**: Early closure simulator, credit card payoff planner.
- **Investment Tracker**: Unified view of SIPs, FDs, PPF, stocks. SIP calculator, goal-linked projections.
- **Finance Blog / Learn**: Context-aware article suggestions, MDX-powered blog, AI-curated reading list.

## Success Criteria

1. **Frictionless logging**: Users can log transactions in under 3 taps or one natural language sentence.
2. **Intelligent insights**: AI provides actionable advice (e.g., "Here's how to close your credit card in 8 months") instead of passive observations.
3. **Responsive and Premium UI**: The app feels responsive and alive, with a vibrant, dynamic design, smooth gradients, and micro-animations.
4. **Trust & Security**: Data is secure, encrypted, and users have complete control over their accounts.
